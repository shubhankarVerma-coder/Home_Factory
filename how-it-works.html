How HomeFactory Works

A simple process that brings income to your home.

1. Sign up

Quick OTP login. Choose languages and your work preferences.

2. Pick a task

View available tasks, payment, and time required.

3. Receive materials

Everything is delivered to your home.

4. Complete work

Follow simple instructions or watch short training videos.

5. Get paid instantly

Money is transferred to your UPI/bank after quick quality check.
