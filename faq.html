Help Center

Common questions answered for new and existing workers.

How do I start working?

Create an account, select a task, and materials will be delivered to your home.

How do I get paid?

After task submission and QC approval, payments are sent instantly via UPI or bank transfer.

Do I need experience?

No experience required. Short training videos are included for every task.

Is there a joining fee?

No, HomeFactory is completely free for workers.
